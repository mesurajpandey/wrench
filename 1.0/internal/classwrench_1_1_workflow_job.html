Member Enumeration Documentation

enum wrench::WorkflowJob::Type

Job types.

Enumerator
STANDARD 

A standard job that can be submitted directly to a ComputeService for execution.

PILOT 

A pilot job that can be submitted to a ComputeService and that, once started, will act as a ComputeService (likely a MultihostMulticoreComputeService) with an expiration date.
